The Base

Not Just a Place
to Sleep

Villa Martial Arts Gym & Hostel is a Toul Tom Poung base for guests who want serious training and practical stays in Phnom Penh. The gym floor, social spaces, and recovery areas are built for people who travel with purpose. Training sets the rhythm of the whole property.

Daily Sessions

Morning and evening classes for Kun Khmer, boxing, and kickboxing with all-level options for visiting guests.

Budget-Friendly Rooms

Shared bunks and private rooms starting from around $6 to $16 depending on room type and availability.

Russian Market Access

Stay in a walkable part of Phnom Penh with cafes, pharmacies, and food spots nearby between sessions.

Fast Booking

Reserve online, then confirm details over WhatsApp if you want the fastest response on room or class availability.

Location & Access

Train in Toul Tom Poung,
Stay Close to Everything

We are based on St 476 in Toul Tom Poung within Chamkar Mon. That puts you close to Russian Market, quick tuk-tuk routes to BKK1, and practical day-to-day access for longer stays in Phnom Penh.

Toul Tom Poung
