The Philosophy

NOT JUST A PLACE

Villa Martial Arts Gym & Hostel is a Toul Tom Poung base for guests who want serious training and practical stays in Phnom Penh. PHNOM PENH FAQ

WHERE IS VILLA MARTIAL ARTS GYM & HOSTEL?

We are located on St 476 in Toul Tom Poung, Chamkar Mon, Phnom Penh. You are close to Russian Market and short rides from BKK1.

ARE KUN KHMER CLASSES BEGINNER FRIENDLY?

Yes. We run all-level sessions for beginners, intermediate guests, and fighter-focused blocks with technical coaching.

HOW MUCH DOES A STAY COST?

Rates commonly start around $6 for dorm options and around $15 for private rooms, depending on dates and availability.
