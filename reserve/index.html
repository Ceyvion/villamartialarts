Before You Book

Where is Villa located?

Villa Martial Arts Gym & Hostel is on St 476 in Toul Tom Poung, Chamkar Mon, Phnom Penh, close to Russian Market.

Are classes beginner friendly?

Yes. The camp runs all-level sessions for beginners, intermediate guests, and more fighter-focused training blocks.

What do rooms cost?

Rates commonly start around $6 for dorm options and around $15 for private rooms, depending on dates and room availability.
